About SkillLinker

Empowering communities through skills, trust, and opportunity since 2020.

Our Story

SkillLinker was founded in 2020 by Kgololosego Moleba, a passionate software engineer who saw the need for a safe, easy way for people to offer their skills and find trusted help in their communities.

Today, SkillLinker is home to over 50,000 members and has helped exchange more than 120,000 services across South Africa and beyond.
